Introduction

This page details the brief history of the Beatle's early 60's live tours in the US. A chronological list of Beatles albums can be accessed from the Albums Gallery page.

Timeline

Formed in Liverpool back in 1960, The Beatles would go on to become arguably the most influential band ever, comprising John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. In the early '60s, they achieved global sensation and moved from pop chart success to innovative albums like Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band. But with success came internal tensions that broke the band up in 1970. Brief as their time together was, the music and the culture they helped create remain enduring and deep.
